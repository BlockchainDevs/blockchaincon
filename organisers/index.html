A brief introduction about us.

Global Fintech Conference 2019, is a Community event a collaboration between Ramanujan College - Delhi University & Different Blockchain Communities from All around India. Namely: BlockchainDevs, HyperLedger Delhi/NCR, ZCash India

  • Ramanujan College

    Ramanujan college is a premier institution of the University of Delhi and has been accredited grade "A" by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). We at Ramanujan College emphasize on the holistic development of personality and meaningful exposure to real world and in the process attain excellence. In this endeavour to organise a value packed Global FinTech Conference, Department of Commerce, Computer Science and Management Studies of Ramanujan College have locked their synergies together.

  • HyperLedger Delhi/NCR

    Hyperledger is an open source collaborative effort created to advance cross-industry blockchain technologies. It is a global collaboration, hosted by The Linux Foundation, including leaders in finance, banking, Internet of Things, supply chains, manufacturing and Technology. Hyperledger Meetup groups have an informal relationship with Hyperledger, and make up a key part of the Hyperledger ecosystem. Participation in a Hyperledger Meetup group is open to anyone--employees of a Hyperledger member company, Hyperledger contributors and developers, and people just passionate about blockchain technology.
