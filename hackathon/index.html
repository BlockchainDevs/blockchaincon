Rules

  1. 75% of you told us you want to hack all night with your colleagues and have fun, so we are opening up the FinHack. Your FinHack submissions needn't be solving a problem for FinTech; we've opened it up. #FTH
  2. Team Size: 3-5 pax. Please register your teams before 20 February 2019 via this registration form.
  3. Hardware entries are welcome. Do note that you have to procure the hardware yourself before the hackathon.
  4. Teams are not allowed to deploy their hacks to staging for the hackathon. Each team has their own AWS account which they can use to spin up necessary infrastructure.

Themes

  • BYOI - Bring Your Own Idea

    If you already have an idea, you can commence the hackathon if your team is successfully registered.

  • Shark Tank Pitches

    If you don't have an idea, don't worry! There will be Shark Tank Pitches for 3 mins by your fellow online team members on 24 February 2019. Pitches will contain business problems that need technological solutions. Here is what a Shark Tank Pitch looks like.

  • Find-Design-Hack

    Don't like the pitches either? We'll give you an anonymized dataset and you can "find" the problem you want to solve.
